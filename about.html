FOR THE LOVE OF CAKE!


Co-founders, Ben Ali & Nadiyah Shahab started the Absolut Chocolat brand out of a common love for baking. Within the first 2 months of operation, the local brand started exporting out its first product to Singapore and Brunei. In 5 months, they grew local operations to have over 15 sales agents serving both East and West Malaysia. The duo also has grown its brand presence to over 50 Petronas outlets in the first year of operations, spreading their love for baking across the nation. All cakes are baked fresh everyday in Kuala Lumpur.
